About

Savepaste is a simple and secure image hosting platform that allows you to paste, view, and download images without storing them on the server.

How to Use

Savepaste allows you to paste an image, which will be displayed on the screen. After pasting, you can also download the image.

FAQs

  • What image formats are supported? PNG, JPEG, and GIF.
  • Are images stored on the server? No, images are only available during the current session.
  • Can I download the pasted images? Yes, you can download the images directly from the browser.
